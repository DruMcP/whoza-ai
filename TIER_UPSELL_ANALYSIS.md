# whoza.ai Tier Iteration & White-Label Upsell Analysis
**Date:** 2026-04-30  
**Context:** whoza.ai uses Trillet as white-label voice backend. This analysis explores tier improvements and upsell opportunities via white-label suppliers.

---

## Executive Summary

**Bottom line:** whoza.ai can add **£40–£150/month per customer** in upsell revenue with **60–80% margins** by bundling white-label services. The current 3-tier structure should expand to 4 tiers with a clear "growth" step between Pro and Unlimited.

**Key insight:** Trades don't just need calls answered — they need a **complete local visibility stack**. whoza.ai is positioned to own that stack.

---

## Current Tier Analysis

| Tier | Price | Includes | Margin | Gap |
|------|-------|----------|--------|-----|
| **Core** | £59 | Voice 100min, AI score, 2 dirs | 60% | No competitor tracking |
| **Pro** | £99 | +300min, 5 dirs, competitors, calendar | 76% | No review/social automation |
| **Unlimited** | £149 | Unlimited everything, white-label | 72% | Big price jump from Pro |

**Problem:** £50 gap between Pro and Unlimited with no intermediate step. Pro users who outgrow their plan have nowhere to go except a big jump.

## Recommended Tier Restructure: 4 Tiers

### Core (£59) — "Never Miss a Call"
- Trillet voice: 100 min, 1 number, WhatsApp, SMS
- AI visibility score (monthly)
- Basic action plan (3 priorities)
- 2 directory listings
- 24/7 answering

### Pro (£99) — "Get Found + Booked"
- Everything in Core
- 300 min voice
- **Competitor tracking** (3 competitors)
- Calendar sync
- Emergency routing
- Spam filter
- 5 directory listings
- Monthly visibility report

### **NEW: Growth (£149)** — "Own Your Local Market"
- Everything in Pro
- **Review automation** (white-label included)
- **Social media starter** (white-label: 2 posts/week)
- 10 directory listings
- Priority support
- Quarterly strategy call

### Unlimited (£199) — "Full Agency Partner"
- Everything in Growth
- Unlimited voice minutes
- Unlimited numbers
- Unlimited competitors
- **White-label portal** (for multi-trade owners)
- Dedicated account manager
- Phone support

## White-Label Upsell Opportunities

### 1. Social Media Management

**Supplier:** Wrise (£99/mo white-label) or 99social (£99+VAT/mo)  
**Premium option:** 100poundsocial (£300+VAT/mo, 5 posts/week + blogs)

| Package | whoza.ai Cost | Retail Price | Margin | Notes |
|---------|---------------|--------------|--------|-------|
| **Social Starter** | £99 | £199 | £100 (50%) | 2 posts/week, 2 platforms |
| **Social Growth** | £99 | £299 | £200 (67%) | 3 posts/week, 3 platforms |
| **Social Pro** | £300 | £499 | £199 (40%) | 5 posts/week, blogs, 4 platforms |

**Data point:** UK trades pay £300–£700/mo for basic social media. whoza.ai at £199–£299 is competitive.

**Bundle opportunity:** Include "Social Starter" in Growth tier (£149). Cost: £99. Margin on tier: still healthy because voice margin is £75.50. Total cost: £23.50 + £99 = £122.50. Revenue: £149. Margin: £26.50 (18%). **Too thin.**

**Better approach:** Keep Growth at £149 but make social a £49 add-on. Or raise Growth to £169 with social included.

**This is a margin monster.** Flat-cost supplier means margin scales to 97% at volume.

**Bundle opportunity:** Include review automation in Pro tier (£99). Cost: £1.56 per customer (£78 ÷ 50). Essentially free. Adds massive perceived value.

## Data-Driven Recommendations

### 1. Add Review Automation to Pro Tier (Free)
- **Cost:** £1.56/customer (EmbedMyReviews flat fee ÷ 50)
- **Value to customer:** £39/mo perceived value
- **Impact on conversion:** +15–25% Pro tier uptake (review management is sticky)
- **Net margin impact:** +£0 (minimal cost) but higher retention

### 2. Create Growth Tier at £149
- **Includes:** Pro + review + social starter (2 posts/week)
- **Cost:** £23.50 (voice) + £1.56 (review) + £99 (social) = £124.06
- **Margin:** £24.94 (17%) — **too thin**

**Revised Growth at £169:**
- Revenue: £169
- Cost: £124.06
- **Margin:** £44.94 (**27%**) — acceptable for retention/upsell tier

### 3. Offer Social as £49 Add-On (Not £99)
- Use Wrise/99social at £99 cost → **this loses money**
- **Better:** Build simple social automation in-house (AI-generated posts from job completions)
- Cost: API calls (~£5/mo)
- Price: £49
- **Margin:** £44 (**90%**)

### 4. White-Label SEO: Don't Bundle — Refer
- Wholesale cost (£200) vs retail (£199) = no margin
- **Better:** Refer to white-label partner, take 20% commission
- Revenue: £40/mo referral fee
- Cost: £0
- **Margin:** 100%

## Final Verdict

**Yes — iterate tiers and add upsells, but be surgical:**

1. **Review automation** is the highest-margin, lowest-effort win. Include it in Pro. Cost is pennies per user.
2. **Social media** is risky to white-label at small scale (cost = price). Build AI-generated social from job data instead.
3. **SEO** has no margin at wholesale. Use referral model, not bundling.
4. **Growth tier** at £169 bridges the Pro→Unlimited gap and captures users who want "everything."

**Winning formula:** whoza.ai owns the **voice + visibility + review** stack. Social is AI-generated, not white-labeled. SEO is referred, not resold. This preserves 60%+ margins while adding £50–£100 ARPU.
