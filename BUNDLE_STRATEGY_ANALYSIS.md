## 1. Executive Summary

**Yes, bundling AI Voice Agent + AI Visibility creates a significant competitive edge.** But only if executed as a **"growth loop"** rather than a feature dump.

**The core logic:** Visibility drives calls. Voice Agent captures calls. Together they form a closed loop: **Get found → Get called → Get booked → Get reviewed → Get found more.**

**Bottom line:** Bundle them. Price the bundle at 15–25% less than separate purchase. This makes whoza.ai the only platform that solves the complete "phone-to-profit" pipeline for UK tradespeople.

## 4. The "Growth Loop" Mechanism

This is the key insight: the two services **reinforce each other**.

```
[AI Visibility]
    ├── Optimised Google Business Profile → more search impressions
    ├── Trade directory listings → more referral traffic
    ├── Review generation → higher local ranking
    └── Better SEO → more organic calls
              |
              v
[AI Voice Agent]
    ├── Captures every call 24/7 → zero missed opportunities
    ├── Books appointments → calendar fills
    ├── Collects caller details → lead database grows
    ├── Follows up with SMS → higher conversion
    └── Asks for review → more 5-star ratings
              |
              v
[Reviews + Activity Data]
    ├── More reviews → higher Google ranking
    ├── More bookings → stronger GBP signals
    ├── Call data reveals demand patterns → visibility strategy refines
    └── More calls → more data → better AI training
              |
              v
    [Back to AI Visibility — loop repeats]
```

**What this means:** Each month a client stays, both services get stronger. The bundle has **increasing returns**, not diminishing.

**Savings vs. separate:**
- Starter: £49 + £39 = £88 → Bundle £79 (**10% off**)

**Why this works:**
- The discount is meaningful enough to drive bundle selection
- But not so steep that it destroys margin
- Pro tier has the deepest discount because that's where you want to push clients (highest LTV)

## 9. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Level | Mitigation |
|------|-------|------------|
| **Complexity** | Medium | Market bundle as single product "whoza.ai Complete" — don't expose the component architecture |
| **Some clients only want voice** | Low | Keep "Voice Only" and "Visibility Only" as hidden options; upsell to bundle in onboarding |
| **Visibility delivery harder to scale** | Medium | Automate directory submissions with APIs; use AI content generation for GBP posts |
| **Clients don't understand visibility value** | Medium | Lead with voice (tangible), show visibility as "the engine that makes it ring" |
| **Higher churn if one component fails** | Low | Monitor both services separately; if voice has issues, visibility keeps client sticky |
