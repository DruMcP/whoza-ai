**Date:** 2026-04-28  
**Market:** UK Tradespeople (Plumbers, Electricians, Builders, HVAC, Roofers, Locksmiths)  
**Analyst:** whoza.ai Strategy

---

## Executive Summary

We identified **10 direct competitors** operating in the UK trades AI voice receptionist space. None offer the **combined AI Voice + AI Visibility bundle** that whoza.ai is positioned to deliver. Our competitive moat is **not** voice technology alone — it is the **growth loop** created by bundling voice capture with visibility amplification.

**Key Finding:** Every competitor is either a **pure voice play** (single product) or a **generic AI receptionist** (not trades-specific). No competitor bundles voice with the traffic source that creates the calls in the first place.

**Competitive Grade:** whoza.ai has a **genuine structural moat** if we execute the bundle. As a standalone voice product, we are competitive but not dominant.

## Where Competitors Are Strong (Respect These)

### 1. Team Connect — Feature Density King
- **Postcode routing** — routes calls to nearest engineer by postcode (we don't have this)
- **Photo request via SMS** — AI sends SMS link for customer to upload photos (we don't have this)
- **SMS Hub** — bulk campaigns, review requests, boiler service reminders (we don't have this)
- **White-label at Enterprise** — they offer it, but only at £99.99/mo tier
- **Extremely cheap entry** — £9.99/mo undercuts everyone

**Response:** Postcode routing and photo requests are **addable features** via Trillet webhooks + whoza.ai dashboard. Not core moat but should be on roadmap. Their cheap entry is a threat — we need to justify our price with bundle value.

### 2. VoiceFleet — Content Marketing Machine
- **Heavy SEO investment** — trades-specific blog content, ROI calculators, demo clips
- **30-day free trial, no credit card** — low friction entry
- **Multilingual agents** — 30+ languages (we have this via Trillet)
- **Strong trust signals** — Trustpilot, G2, Capterra claimed
- **Regional deployment** — EU data residency (GDPR advantage)

**Response:** Their content marketing is a benchmark we should exceed. Our blog + SEO is already operational — we need to out-publish them on trades-specific pain points.

### 3. CallChimps — The "Human Touch" Positioning
- **Positioning:** "Professional, UK-based, human touch" — they lean into AI + warmth
- **Cold caller identification** — screens spam/sales calls (useful)
- **Mobile app** — tradespeople check summaries on phone
- **Custom LLM training** — can train on historic calls

**Response:** Their "human touch" positioning is defensive — implies AI alone is cold. Our counter: **Trillet's voices are indistinguishable from human** (ElevenLabs tier). We should demo this aggressively.

### 4. AlconBlu.ai — The Honest Broker
- **Founder is a qualified electrician** — unbeatable credibility in trades
- **Honest about AI limitations** — discloses 15-20% hang-up rate (builds trust)
- **Trades language understanding** — knows the difference between emergency and routine
- **"Built by someone who was under a sink"** — authentic positioning

**Response:** This is the **most credible competitor** for trades trust. Our counter is **scale + bundle** — they can't compete with our visibility engine, but we need to match their authenticity in messaging.

**Validation:**
- No competitor offers bundled visibility

**Durability:** HIGH — requires competitor to build entirely new product category

**Durability:** MEDIUM — Trillet relationship is replicable if competitor finds similar partner

**Description:** Every call transcript, every job type, every postcode captured **trains our understanding** of trade customer patterns.

- We can aggregate across hundreds of trade businesses
- Data improves AI training, conversion optimisation, and content marketing

**Durability:** HIGH — data compound interest; more clients = better AI = more clients

### Moat #4: SEO/Content Authority
**Description:** Our existing 95-page website + blog + schema markup gives us **organic reach** that voice-only competitors lack.

**Validation:**
- VoiceFleet is investing in content but lacks visibility product
