---

## 1. How the 300-Minute Pool Works

The Agency plan includes **300 minutes across ALL client sub-accounts combined** — not per client. This pool gets consumed first, then overage kicks in.

### Example: 10 clients using 500 min each = 5,000 total minutes

| Tier | Minutes | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| Included pool | 300 min | $0 (part of $299) |
| Overage | 4,700 min | 4,700 × $0.09 = **$423** |
| **Total Trillet bill** | 5,000 min | **$722/mo** ($299 + $423) |
| **Effective rate/min** | — | **$0.144/min** |

**Critical insight:** At low client counts, the 300-min pool gives you a discount. At scale, it becomes negligible and your effective rate approaches $0.09/min.

## 7. Risk Scenarios

### What if Trillet raises prices?

| Change | Impact on 30-client model |
|--------|---------------------------|
| Platform fee ↑ to $399 | -$100/mo profit (5.8% margin hit) |
| Overage ↑ to $0.12/min | -$366/mo profit (21% margin hit) |
| Both ↑ | -$466/mo profit (27% margin hit) |

### What if clients use more minutes than expected?

| Over-usage | Impact on 30-client model |
|------------|---------------------------|
| +20% usage (22,320 min) | -$165/mo profit |
| +50% usage (27,900 min) | -$412/mo profit |
| Double usage (37,200 min) | -$1,650/mo profit |

**Mitigation:** Build overage clauses into whoza.ai pricing. E.g., "Includes 500 min, then £0.15/min overage" — you charge more than your $0.09 cost.

## 9. Key Takeaways

1. **The 300-min pool is a small sweetener** — saves ~$27/mo. At scale, it's irrelevant.
2. **Break-even is ~5 clients** mixed across Starter/Growth tiers.
3. **Target 40%+ net margin** once you hit 15+ clients.
4. **At 100 clients, you're making ~$77K/year** with minimal operational overhead (Trillet handles all voice infra).
5. **Protect yourself:** Add overage charges to client pricing. Your cost is $0.09/min; charge them £0.12–£0.15/min.
6. **Annual contract with Trillet:** Lock in $299/mo before any price increases.

---

**Bottom line:** The P&L is healthy. At 30 clients you're pulling £1,360/mo net profit. At 100 clients, it's £5,100/mo. The 300-min pool helps early but doesn't materially change the model at scale.
