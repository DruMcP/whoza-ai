# Research — Competitive Pricing Analysis

## Summary
whoza.ai's proposed pricing (£49–£499/mo) sits in the "premium AI" bracket — more than bare-bones AI tools (£20–40/mo) but dramatically cheaper than human virtual receptionists (£99–250/mo). Position against human receptionists, not cheap AI tools.

## UK Market Pricing Tiers

### Human Virtual Receptionists (Incumbents)
| Competitor | Monthly Cost | Included | Overage | 24/7 Cost |
|------------|-------------|----------|---------|-----------|
| **Moneypenny** | £99/mo | 30 min | £1.65–£1.85/min | +£75/mo extra |
| **AllDayPA** | £210/mo | 150 min | £1.00/min | Included |
| **AnswerConnect** | £245/mo | 150 min | £1.75/min | Included |
| **Ruby Receptionist** | ~£185/mo | Limited | ~£1.50/min | Varies |
| **VoiceNation** | Pay-per-min | — | ~£0.80/min | Included |

**Key insight:** Human services cost **£1.00–£1.85 per minute** in overage. whoza.ai's cost is ~£0.07/min. Even at highest client overage charge (£0.15/min), we're **6–12x cheaper** than human receptionists.
