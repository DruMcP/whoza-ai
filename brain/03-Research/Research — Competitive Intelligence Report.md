# Research — Competitive Intelligence Report

## Summary
10 direct competitors identified in UK trades AI voice receptionist space. None offer the combined AI Voice + AI Visibility bundle. whoza.ai's moat is the **growth loop**: visibility drives calls, voice captures calls, reviews amplify visibility.

## Key Threats to Respect
1. **Team Connect** — Feature density king (postcode routing, photo requests, SMS Hub, white-label at £99.99)
2. **VoiceFleet** — Content marketing machine (heavy SEO, ROI calculators, demo clips, 30-day trial)
3. **CallChimps** — "Human touch" positioning (UK-based warmth, cold caller ID, mobile app)

## Strategic Response
- Out-publish VoiceFleet on trades-specific pain points
- Add postcode routing and photo requests to roadmap (doable via Trillet webhooks)
- Lean into bundle as the only true differentiator
- Price against human receptionists, not cheap AI tools
