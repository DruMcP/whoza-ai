# Research — Trillet Deep Dive

## Summary
Trillet is the chosen base for whoza.ai's voice agent offering. They have a mature UK-focused "Trade Receptionist" product with call divert, number management, and usage-based pricing. We are replicating their feature set under our own brand.

## Key Data
- **Product:** Trade Receptionist (exists today)
- **Features:** Call answering, message taking, call divert, call transfer, out-of-hours handling
- **Numbers:** Can supply UK local/03 numbers; call divert from client existing numbers is a core feature
- **Pricing:** Per-minute + monthly service fee; minute bundles available with overage
- **Integration:** API available for white-label partners
- **Geographic focus:** UK

## Implications
Trillet is the fastest path to market. Their existing feature set maps closely to whoza.ai's target SME offer. The key negotiation is white-label terms (branding, support split, margin).

## Open Questions
1. Exact white-label contract terms — legal review needed
2. API rate limits at scale
3. SLA guarantees for voice uptime
4. Can we resell their number rental or must clients bring their own?
