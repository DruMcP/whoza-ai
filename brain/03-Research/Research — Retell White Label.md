# Research — Retell White Label

## Summary
Retell AI is the backup voice platform for whoza.ai. Offers per-client white-label with no upfront fees — viable if Trillet cannot meet terms for a specific client.

## Key Findings
- **White-label:** Yes — fully customizable voice, persona, scripts per client
- **UK Telephony:** Yes — provisions UK numbers directly or via Twilio integration
- **Cost:** ~£0.08–£0.16/min all-in (infrastructure + voice + LLM + telephony)
- **Strengths:** Best-in-class voice quality (Cartesia/ElevenLabs), built-in telephony layer, real-time web dashboard
- **Reseller Fit:** Excellent — can rebrand dashboard and voice experience per client
- **No upfront fees:** Can white-label client-by-client without agency plan commitment

## Comparison to Trillet
| Dimension | Trillet | Retell |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Cost | $0.09/min | £0.08–0.16/min |
| White-label | Agency plan required | Per-client, no upfront |
| UK focus | Strong | Good |
| Voice quality | Good | Excellent |
| Trade-specific features | "Trade Receptionist" exists | Generic, customizable |

## When to Use Retell
- Trillet white-label terms unfavorable for a specific client
- Client needs premium voice quality (Cartesia)
- whoza.ai wants to avoid Trillet Agency Plan commitment initially
