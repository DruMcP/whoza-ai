# Research — Client Offer Overview

## Summary
whoza.ai offers the **Complete AI Growth Platform for UK Tradespeople** — combining AI Visibility (get found) + AI Voice Agent (capture every call) in one platform, one price, zero missed opportunities.

## The Problem
Every UK tradesperson knows the same pain:
- Up a ladder or under a sink — phone rings, missed. By the time they call back, customer hired someone else.
- Competitor answers first — for emergency trades, first person to pick up wins 85% of the time.
- Spend money on listings (Checkatrade, Yell, Google Ads) — no idea if they're working.
- Evenings spent returning calls — instead of being with family.

**Average UK tradesperson loses £24,000/year to missed calls.**

## What You Get

### 1. AI Voice Agent — Your 24/7 Receptionist
- Answers every call in your business name with a natural British voice
- Books jobs straight into your diary (Google Calendar, Outlook, ServiceM8)
- Qualifies enquiries: postcode, job type, urgency, budget
- Handles emergencies: "burst pipe" triggers instant mobile transfer
- Filters spam: PPI calls, cold callers blocked automatically
- Sends summaries: WhatsApp or SMS after every call
- Works with your existing number: simple call divert, no number change

**Setup time:** 10–15 minutes. No technical skills needed.

### 2. AI Visibility — Get Found Before Your Competitors
- Google Business Profile optimisation
- Trade directory listings (15–30 directories)
- Review generation (automatic post-job requests)
- Local SEO content ("plumber Manchester" etc.)
- Competitor tracking
- Keyword monitoring

### 3. The Growth Loop
```
AI Visibility → more search impressions → more calls
AI Voice Agent → answers every call → books every job
Reviews → boost ranking → more visibility
```

Every month on whoza.ai, both services get stronger. Compounding investment in pipeline.
