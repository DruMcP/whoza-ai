# 00-Inbox — Raw Captures

## What Goes Here
Fleeting notes, quick ideas, raw research snippets, half-formed thoughts. Anything that doesn't have a home yet.

## Triage Rules
1. **Daily:** Review inbox. Move notes to proper folders or delete.
2. **Format:** Even a raw note should have `created:` and `tags:` frontmatter.
3. **No orphaning:** If you dump something here, either process it or delete it within 48h.
