# Reference — Validated Business Model

## Summary
whoza.ai operates as a white-label voice agent reseller + AI visibility platform. We package technical partners' infrastructure, add our brand, onboarding, and support, and sell to UK SMEs.

## Revenue Streams
1. **Voice Agent SaaS** — Monthly per-client fee + usage (minutes)
2. **AI Visibility Platform** — Monthly SaaS for ChatGPT/Perplexity visibility
3. **Bundles** — Combined voice + visibility at tiered pricing

## Cost Structure
- **COGS:** Trillet/Retell usage, number rental, telecom costs
- **Engineering:** Netlify + Supabase + Vercel (low at current scale)
- **Acquisition:** Organic (SEO) + limited paid (test budget)
- **Support:** Self-service + agent-assisted

## Unit Economics
See [[TRILLET_PnL_MODEL.md]] for detailed forecasts.
Key target: 40%+ gross margin on voice bundles after COGS.

## Strategic Assumptions
- UK SME market is underserved for AI voice + AI visibility
- White-label keeps capital requirements low
- Bundle pricing increases LTV and reduces churn
- Voice agent acts as wedge; visibility platform deepens relationship
