North Star: Whoza gives every UK trades business an AI revenue team that answers calls, captures enquiries, builds reputation, watches competitors and tells them what to do next to win more work.

1. What Whoza.ai Will Be at Launch

Whoza.ai will launch as a subscription plus usage-based AI revenue system for UK tradespeople and local service businesses.

It should not be positioned as a generic AI receptionist. It should be positioned as the AI Revenue Team for UK Trades.

The business will combine AI call answering, enquiry qualification, WhatsApp/SMS/email delivery, booking support, review collection, competitor tracking, AI visibility recommendations, dashboards and optional automation consultancy.
