# US AI Search Research for Blog Post

## Key Statistics (January 2026)

### AI Search Usage in US
- **57% of homeowners** now ask ChatGPT "Who should I hire?" for contractors
- **ChatGPT users doubled** from 400M to 800M in just 8 months
- **AI search predicted to overtake Google** by 2028 (23 months away)
- **ChatGPT has ~75%** of US AI chatbot market share
- **96% still use Google** but now combine with AI platforms

### Where US Homeowners Search for Contractors
- 96% use Google
- 90% watch YouTube for cost breakdowns and before/after videos
- 80% ask on Reddit "Has anyone used a contractor in my city?"
- 62% use Yahoo (19% above national average)
- 57% ask ChatGPT for recommendations

### AI Visibility Challenge
- AI visibility is **3-30x harder** than traditional Google ranking
- Only **1.2% of locations** are recommended by ChatGPT vs 35.9% in Google local 3-pack
- AI favors businesses with **4.3+ star ratings** and consistent data

### Key AEO Tactics for US Contractors
1. **Schema Markup** - FAQ schema tells AI what's a question/answer
2. **Question-Based Headers** - "How Much Does X Cost in [City]?"
3. **40-60 Word Answer Formula** - Perfect length for AI extraction
4. **Multi-Platform Presence** - YouTube, Reddit, LinkedIn, not just website
5. **Content Multiplication** - Repurpose one blog into 15+ touchpoints

### US-Specific Considerations
- Reference US licensing (state contractor licenses, EPA certifications)
- Use US terminology (HVAC, not "heating engineers")
- Reference US platforms (Yelp, Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack)
- Include US cities and regions
- Use USD pricing examples
