## 🚨 CRITICAL ISSUE IDENTIFIED

The Solution section in case studies was giving away **12 weeks of paid actionable advice** for free. This represented a direct leakage of £19/month value proposition.

### What Was Being Given Away:

**Before (FREE):**
- ✅ "Complete Google Business profile with all service details"
- ✅ "Add high-quality photos of recent work"
- ✅ "Set up review request system"
- ✅ "Add FAQ section to website addressing common plumbing questions"
- ✅ "Create service area pages for Birmingham neighborhoods"
- ✅ "Encourage satisfied customers to leave detailed reviews"
- ✅ "Add certifications and insurance details"
- ✅ "Optimize for local AI search queries"

**Problem:** Anyone could read these case studies and execute the entire 12-week roadmap without paying a single penny. This undermines the core value proposition of the platform.

---

## ✅ SOLUTION IMPLEMENTED

Completely redrafted all Solution sections across all 3 case studies to:

1. **Remove specific tactical advice** - No more step-by-step instructions
2. **Focus on outcomes, not actions** - WHAT improved, not HOW
3. **Tease the platform's value** - Hint at personalized guidance behind paywall
4. **Maintain credibility** - Still demonstrates the platform works

### Content Strategy:

1. **Show outcomes, not tactics**

   - Transformation > Transaction
   - Value demonstration > Value delivery

2. **Tease, don't teach**
   - Hint at platform intelligence
   - Emphasize personalization
   - Create curiosity gap

3. **Platform as protagonist**
   - "Rex guided them through..."
   - "The platform provided..."
   - "Automatically prioritizing..."

1. **Audit all public content**

   - Case studies

   - Prioritization
