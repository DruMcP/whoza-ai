## 1. Executive Summary

**Bottom line:** whoza.ai's proposed pricing (£49–£499/mo) is **competitive but not the cheapest**. It sits in the "premium AI" bracket — more expensive than bare-bones AI tools (£20–£40/mo) but **dramatically cheaper** than human virtual receptionists (£99–£250/mo). The key competitive advantage is **trade-specific specialization** + **call divert from existing number** — something most AI competitors don't natively support.

**Positioning recommendation:** Price against human receptionist services (Moneypenny, AnswerConnect), not against cheap AI tools. Your value proposition is "human-quality call handling at AI prices."

## 7. Recommended Competitive Messaging

### Against Human Services (Moneypenny, AnswerConnect, AllDayPA)

**Headline:** *"The receptionist who never sleeps, never takes a holiday, and costs 80% less."*

**Key messages:**
- Moneypenny charges £1.65/min for extra calls. We include 300–3,000 minutes.
- AnswerConnect takes 4 rings to answer. We answer in under 2 seconds.
- Human services need 3–6 month contracts. We're monthly.
- Human receptionists cost £75 extra for 24/7. We're 24/7 on every plan.

### Against Cheap AI Tools (CallChimps, Dialzara, Ring Eden)

**Headline:** *"Generic AI costs you jobs. Trade-trained AI books them."*

**Key messages:**
- CallChimps overage is £0.97/min. Ours is £0.15/min.
- Cheap AI can't tell a burst pipe from a leaky tap. We can.
- Generic tools don't understand "emergency call escalation." We do.
- Our AI knows your trade vocabulary (flashing, soffit, consumer unit, stopcock).

### Against Hybrid (Smith.ai, GoodCall)

**Headline:** *"Why pay for human backup when the AI handles 95% of calls perfectly?"*

**Key messages:**
- Smith.ai hybrid costs £230/mo. Our Pro is £199 with no human handoff delays.
- Hybrid = AI triage + human queue. Pure AI = instant resolution.
- Your callers don't want to be transferred. They want their problem solved.

### Recommended: Keep Current Tiers, Tweak Messaging

| Plan | Price | Positioning | Adjustment Needed |
|------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Starter** | £49 | Solo tradesperson | ✅ Keep — anchors low, beats Moneypenny |
| **Growth** | £99 | Small team | ✅ Keep — the "no-brainer" tier |
| **Pro** | £199 | Established business | ✅ Keep — competes with hybrid services |
| **Elite** | £499 | Multi-location | ⚠️ Consider £349–£399 | High anchor, rarely bought |

### Elite Tier Question Mark

£499/mo is **very high** for AI-only service. Compare:
- Smith.ai hybrid: £230/mo
- AnswerConnect: £245/mo
- Even Moneypenny premium: ~£250/mo

**Options:**
1. **Keep £499** as an anchor to make £199 look cheap (pricing psychology)
2. **Drop to £349** to be "premium but not absurd"
3. **Add human handoff** at £499 to justify the price (hybrid model)

**Recommendation:** Keep £499 as an **anchor/decoy** tier. Most sales will be £99 Growth. The £499 makes £99 look like a bargain.
